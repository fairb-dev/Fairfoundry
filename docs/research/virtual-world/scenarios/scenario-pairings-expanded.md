### S051

**OEM:** Titan Global Mining Corp (OEM-26)
**Factory:** Chennai Precision Forgings Ltd. (Factory 13)
**Product/Service:** High-manganese steel crusher jaw plates (Mn 11-14%, Hadfield steel equivalent) for underground gold mine crushing circuit
**Development Stage:** MP
**Service Order Value:** $380,000
**Test Scenario Reference:** Scenario 29 -- Brinell Hardness Testing (adapted for manganese steel castings)

**Key Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Manganese content 11.0-14.0% per ASTM A128 Grade C
2. Carbon content 1.00-1.35%
3. As-cast hardness HBW 190-230
4. Charpy impact >= 100 J at 25 C (manganese steel must be tough, not brittle)
5. Dimensional tolerance +/- 2.0 mm on all critical crusher interface surfaces

**Expected Outcome:** 94% lot pass rate (manganese steel casting is well-established at Chennai)
**Dispute Likelihood:** Medium -- Titan operates the mine in Ghana; Chennai casts in India; quality data traverses 3 time zones and 2 languages. Crusher jaw plate wear rate data from the mine (operational performance) is never systematically fed back to the casting supplier (manufacturing quality). When wear life is shorter than expected, the dispute defaults to "was the chemistry right?" with no operational context.
**Settlement Timeline:** Week 1-4: Casting production at Chennai. Week 5: Chemistry, hardness, and dimensional testing. Week 6: MTC and shipment (sea freight to Tema, Ghana, 3-4 weeks). Week 10: Arrival and installation at mine site. Week 12: Payment release upon installation acceptance. Total: 10-12 weeks (including transit).

### S052

**OEM:** Volante Automotive Group (OEM-27)
**Factory:** Pune AutoStamp Industries Pvt. Ltd. (Factory 10)
**Product/Service:** Body-in-white stamped door inner panels (zinc-coated HSLA 340 MPa steel) for Volante's Indian-market SUV program
**Development Stage:** PVT
**Service Order Value:** $890,000
**Test Scenario Reference:** Scenario 14 -- Engine Block Dimensional Inspection (adapted for large body panel surface waviness and GD&T)

At $890K per order and $62B OEM revenue, this is a high-stakes production ramp with tight SOP (Start of Production) deadlines. Surface quality disputes can delay SOP by weeks.
**Settlement Timeline:** Week 1-3: PVT lot of 2,000 door inners stamped. Week 4: Dimensional and surface quality inspection. Week 5: PPAP package submission. Week 6: Shipment (surface panels require protective packaging). Week 7-8: Volante incoming inspection at Indian assembly plant. Week 9: If surface waviness disagreement, joint assessment with 3D scanner (GOM ATOS). Week 10: PPAP disposition and payment release. Total: 7-10 weeks.
